Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell.

Dogs can actually smell time! Their incredible sense of smell lets them detect how scents fade throughout the day.Dogs use their sense of smell to find their way home and recognize their owners even after they have been exposed to other scents.

Dogs are incredibly fast runners.

Yes, dogs are incredibly fast runners due to their flexible spines, long legs, and lack of collarbones, which allow for greater stride length and flexibility. While an average dog can run 15–20 mph, specific breeds are much faster, with the Greyhound being the fastest at about 45 mph.

Dogs Dream Just Like Humans

Dogs dream like humans, and they have similar REM sleep patterns, which include brain activity, rapid eye movements, and muscle twitches. Scientists believe their dreams are likely based on their daily experiences, such as playing with their owners, chasing squirrels, or going on walks.

Dogs can learn hundreds of words

Some dogs can understand more than 200 words and gestures—similar to the intelligence of a toddler.
